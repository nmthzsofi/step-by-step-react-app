## Long Description

Every step takes you somewhere. Lépésről Lépésre turns your everyday physical activity into a virtual adventure across the real world map.

Choose any two places on Earth as your start and finish — a weekend hike, a cross-country trek, or even a walk from Budapest to Paris. Log your workouts and watch your progress marker travel the route, kilometre by kilometre.

**Track your movement your way**
Manually log walking, running, cycling, swimming, or tennis sessions. Enter the duration and intensity, and the app converts your effort into steps and distance. Simple, fast, and no wearable required.

**Go further together**
Create group journeys and invite friends using a unique share code. Race each other to the finish line, or combine your steps in cooperative mode to reach a shared destination as a team. A live leaderboard keeps everyone motivated.

**Watch your journey unfold**
An interactive map shows exactly where you are along your chosen route. Your progress marker moves in real time as you log activities — bringing your journey to life with every session.

**Celebrate every milestone**
Earn badges as your lifetime step count grows. Complete a journey and the app celebrates with you. Finish a race first and claim the champion title.

**Privacy first**
No GPS tracking. No ads. No third-party analytics. Your data stays yours.

Whether you are building a daily movement habit, training for a goal, or just looking for a fun way to stay active with friends — Lépésről Lépésre makes every step count.

Start your journey today.
